# Eight Sleep Platform

**The Outcome Layer for Every Health App**

A product vision prototype demonstrating how Eight Sleep can become the platform that closes the feedback loop for the entire health ecosystem.

## The Thesis

Health apps today are guessing at outcomes. Calm can't prove meditation improves your sleep. Strava can't prove your evening run helped recovery. Every health app is flying blind because they don't have continuous, passive, clinical-grade outcome data.

Eight Sleep fixes that. The Pod measures the ground truth every single night. Open that data as a two-way API, and every health app in the world gets smarter — while Eight Sleep builds the largest dataset of "what actually affects human health."

## The 6-Step Platform Journey

1. **Inbound Data Layer** — External context flows in (already happening via Terra/Apple Health)
2. **Outcome Attribution API** — Partner apps query sleep outcomes of their interventions
3. **Closed-Loop Feedback** — Apps adjust recommendations based on outcomes
4. **Pod as Actuator** — Trusted partners get scoped write access to Pod controls
5. **Health Intelligence Network** — Cross-app data creates an unassailable moat
6. **Monetization Layer** — Revenue share, enterprise API, cohort data licensing
